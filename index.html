The PyleaMUN Handbook

Dear Delegates and fellow Advisors,

On this page you will find information regarding your participation to the PyleaMUN Conference. You might be an experienced Delegate or even Student Officer but you might also be a not-so-experienced or even inexperienced Delegate venturing an MUN Conference for the very first time. This Guide has been made for all Delegates, experienced or not, aiming at providing everybody with an opportunity to shine brightly.

The Handbook will guide you step by step and if any of your questions are not answered here or on our site we are only one click away.
